Frequently Asked Questions

What kind of clients do you serve?

Blockchain.com serves a wide range of clients, from crypto-native funds to traditional investors entering the space, as well as family offices and sophisticated or accredited investors.

What safeguards do you have for clients?

Blockchain.com (Singapore) Pte. Ltd. is regulated by the Monetary Authority of Singapore. Other Blockchain.com entities are registered or licensed, where appropriate. As part of the Blockchain.com group, each legal entity is subject to regular audits. Blockchain.com also maintains insurance policies to protect client assets against cyber security threats and theft.

How long does it take to get started?

Onboarding can range from a few days to about one week. Our team works around the clock and reviews documents upon submission to give you the fastest KYC experience possible.
